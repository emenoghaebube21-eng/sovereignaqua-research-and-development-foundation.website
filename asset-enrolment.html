About the Program

The Asset Enrolment Program provides a structured process for documenting eligible assets submitted to the Foundation for participation in approved programs. Applicants should review all eligibility requirements and provide accurate supporting documentation during the enrolment process.

Eligibility Requirements

Identity Verification

Provide valid identification where required.

Ownership Documentation

Submit documentation demonstrating your authority or ownership.

Supporting Records

Include all requested supporting documents for review.

Asset Enrolment Form

Required Documentation

  • Government-issued identification (if applicable)
  • Proof of ownership or authority
  • Supporting records relevant to the asset
  • Completed enrolment form
  • Any additional documentation requested during review

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the review process take?

Review times vary depending on the completeness of the submitted information and the nature of the application.

Can I update my application?

Yes. If additional information is requested, you may submit updates during the review process.

Need Assistance?

If you have questions about eligibility, documentation, or the enrolment process, our team is available to assist you.
