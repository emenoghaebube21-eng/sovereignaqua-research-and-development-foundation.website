MEMBERSHIP CONTRIBUTIONS

Membership Fees & Contribution Schedule

Membership contributions support the Foundation's research initiatives, educational programmes, environmental conservation projects, community outreach, digital resources, publications, and long-term organizational sustainability.

Contribution Schedule

The Foundation's official membership contribution schedule is currently under review by the Board of Trustees. Until formally adopted, prospective members are encouraged to submit their applications. Approved applicants will receive the current contribution schedule during the onboarding process.

Scholarships & Fee Assistance

Subject to available funding, the Foundation may provide scholarships, sponsorships, or contribution assistance for eligible students, volunteers, researchers, and community participants.

Payment Options

Approved payment methods may include secure online payment, bank transfer, institutional invoicing, grant funding, and other Board-approved contribution methods.

Renewals

Membership renewal requirements and any applicable annual contributions will be communicated to members before the commencement of each renewal cycle.

Ready to Join the Foundation?

We encourage interested individuals and organizations to complete their membership application. Contribution details will be provided to successful applicants as part of the membership onboarding process.
