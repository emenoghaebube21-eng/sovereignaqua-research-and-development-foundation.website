ACCESSIBILITY

Accessibility Statement

SovereignAqua Research & Development Foundation is committed to providing an inclusive, accessible, and user-friendly digital experience for everyone, regardless of ability, assistive technology, or browsing device.

OUR COMMITMENT

Inclusive Access for Every Visitor

Accessibility is a core principle of the Foundation. We are committed to designing, developing, and maintaining digital experiences that are inclusive, understandable, and usable by all visitors, including people with disabilities.

We continuously review our website and digital services to improve accessibility while supporting our mission of advancing research, education, sustainability, innovation, and humanitarian development.

PRINCIPLES

Our Accessibility Principles

Inclusive Design

Accessibility is integrated into our design and development process from the outset.

Keyboard Navigation

Interactive elements are designed to be usable without requiring a mouse.

Responsive Experience

Content adapts to desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

STANDARDS

Accessibility Standards We Follow

We aim to conform as closely as reasonably practical with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA, which provide internationally recognized guidance for creating accessible web content.

We also apply modern HTML5 semantics, WAI-ARIA techniques where appropriate, responsive design principles, and accessibility best practices throughout the development lifecycle.

FEATURES

Accessibility Features

Semantic HTML

Our website uses semantic HTML5 elements to provide meaningful page structure for browsers, assistive technologies, and search engines.

Keyboard Navigation

Interactive controls are designed to be accessible using keyboard navigation without requiring a mouse.

Readable Content

Typography, spacing, and colour contrast are selected to improve readability across devices.

Responsive Design

Pages automatically adapt to desktop, tablet, and mobile screen sizes.

Alternative Text

Meaningful images include descriptive alternative text to support screen readers.

Reduced Motion

Where practical, animations respect user preferences for reduced motion.

COMPATIBILITY

Assistive Technology Support

We strive to ensure compatibility with commonly used assistive technologies and modern web browsers, including screen readers, keyboard navigation tools, magnification software, and voice input technologies.

  • Screen reader compatibility where practical.
  • Keyboard-only navigation support.
  • Logical page structure and heading hierarchy.
  • Clearly labelled interactive elements.
  • Responsive layouts across modern devices.
QUALITY ASSURANCE

Accessibility Testing

Accessibility is considered throughout our design, development, and publishing processes. We periodically review website content and functionality to identify opportunities for improvement.

Our review process may include manual inspections, keyboard navigation testing, automated accessibility evaluation tools, and testing across multiple browsers and devices.

THIRD-PARTY CONTENT

External Services

Some areas of this website may include content, documents, or services provided by third parties. While we encourage accessible practices, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of external websites or embedded services that are outside our direct control.

ALTERNATIVE FORMATS

Requesting Accessible Information

If you require information from this website in an alternative format, please contact us. We will make reasonable efforts to provide information in an accessible format whenever practicable.

KNOWN LIMITATIONS

Areas Under Review

We continuously improve the accessibility of our website. Some archived documents, legacy content, downloadable files, or third-party resources may not yet fully meet our accessibility objectives.

As these resources are reviewed or updated, we aim to improve their accessibility wherever feasible.

FEEDBACK

Accessibility Feedback & Assistance

We welcome feedback regarding the accessibility and usability of our website. If you encounter an accessibility barrier or require assistance accessing any content, we encourage you to contact us.

Response Goal

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

Our Ongoing Commitment

Accessibility is an ongoing process rather than a one-time achievement. We regularly review our website, evaluate new content, improve existing resources, and incorporate accessibility best practices into future development.

As technologies, standards, and user needs evolve, we remain committed to improving the accessibility, usability, and inclusiveness of our digital services.

REVIEW

Statement Review

This Accessibility Statement is reviewed periodically and updated whenever significant improvements are made to the website or relevant accessibility standards change.
