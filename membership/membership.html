FOUNDATION MEMBERSHIP

Join a Community Dedicated to Research, Innovation & Sustainable Development

Membership in the SovereignAqua Research & Development Foundation offers individuals, professionals, institutions, and volunteers the opportunity to contribute to scientific research, environmental stewardship, education, innovation, and community development. Members become part of a collaborative network committed to creating meaningful and lasting impact.

MEMBERSHIP CATEGORIES

Choose the Membership That Fits You

Student Member

Designed for undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral students seeking research experience, mentorship, and professional growth.

Professional Member

Ideal for researchers, educators, engineers, consultants, scientists, and industry professionals.

Institutional Member

Universities, NGOs, government agencies, research institutes, and private organizations committed to collaborative partnerships.

Volunteer Member

Individuals committed to supporting Foundation programmes through community service and volunteer engagement.

Supporting Member

Individuals and organizations who wish to advance the Foundation's mission through financial or in-kind support.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can become a member?

Membership is open to individuals and organizations whose interests align with the Foundation's mission and objectives.

Is membership available internationally?

Yes. Applications are welcomed from eligible individuals and organizations worldwide.

How do I renew my membership?

Renewal information will be communicated to members before the applicable renewal period.

Will I receive a membership certificate?

Eligible members may receive a digital or printed membership certificate following successful registration.
