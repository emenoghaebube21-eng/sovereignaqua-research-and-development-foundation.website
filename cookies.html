Cookie Policy

Learn how SovereignAqua Research & Development Foundation uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function efficiently, remember user preferences, improve performance, and provide insights into how visitors use the website.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies – Required for website functionality.
  • Performance Cookies – Help us understand visitor behavior.
  • Functional Cookies – Remember your preferences.
  • Security Cookies – Help protect the website against abuse.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control or delete cookies through their settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website.
