EXPECTATIONS

Our Expectations of Volunteers

Volunteers play an important role in advancing the Foundation's mission. We ask every volunteer to demonstrate professionalism, reliability, respect, and a commitment to ethical service.

Reliability

Honor agreed commitments, communicate promptly, and notify coordinators if circumstances prevent participation.

Professionalism

Represent the Foundation respectfully in all interactions with members, partners, and the public.

Teamwork

Collaborate effectively, support fellow volunteers, and contribute positively to Foundation activities.

CODE OF CONDUCT

Volunteer Standards

  • Respect every individual regardless of background or experience.
  • Act honestly, ethically, and responsibly.
  • Protect confidential information entrusted to you.
  • Follow Foundation safety procedures during activities.
  • Use Foundation resources responsibly.
  • Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose them where appropriate.
  • Support an inclusive, welcoming, and collaborative environment.

FAQ

Volunteer Questions

Who can volunteer?

Individuals who support the Foundation's mission and meet the requirements of a specific volunteer role may apply.

Is volunteering paid?

Volunteer roles are generally unpaid unless explicitly stated in a specific programme or opportunity.

Can international volunteers participate?

Yes. Some volunteer opportunities may be available remotely or internationally, depending on programme needs.

Will I receive training?

Orientation and role-specific guidance may be provided where appropriate for the volunteer assignment.
