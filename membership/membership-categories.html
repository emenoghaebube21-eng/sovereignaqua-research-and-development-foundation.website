OUR MEMBERSHIP

Membership Designed for Every Stage of Your Journey

The Foundation welcomes individuals and organizations from diverse backgrounds who share a commitment to scientific research, environmental sustainability, innovation, education, and community development. Each membership category is tailored to encourage participation, professional growth, and meaningful collaboration.

ADDITIONAL MEMBERSHIPS

More Ways to Engage

Beyond our core membership categories, the Foundation offers additional pathways for individuals and organizations to support our mission according to their experience, contributions, and interests.

Volunteer Membership

For individuals who wish to contribute their time, knowledge, and skills through Foundation programmes, outreach initiatives, and community service activities.

Honorary Membership

Awarded by the Foundation to individuals whose distinguished service, achievements, or contributions significantly advance scientific research, education, sustainability, or humanitarian development.

Supporting Membership

ELIGIBILITY

Who Can Apply?

Individuals

Students, educators, researchers, scientists, professionals, innovators, and community leaders who support the Foundation's mission.

Organizations

Universities, colleges, nonprofit organizations, government agencies, research institutes, and private-sector organizations.

Commitment

APPLICATION PROCESS

How to Become a Member

Step 1

Choose the membership category that best matches your qualifications and interests.

Step 2

Complete the online membership application form and submit any required supporting documents.

Step 3

Your application will be reviewed according to the Foundation's membership policies and procedures.

Step 4

Approved applicants will receive a welcome package with information about accessing Foundation programmes and resources.

RESPONSIBILITIES

Our Shared Commitment

Membership is more than a privilege—it is a commitment to uphold the Foundation's values, contribute positively to the research community, and support ethical, inclusive, and sustainable practices.

Integrity

Demonstrate honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all Foundation activities.

Respect

Foster an inclusive environment by respecting diverse perspectives, cultures, and professional backgrounds.

Sustainability

Promote responsible environmental stewardship and support initiatives that contribute to sustainable development.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Membership Questions

Can I change my membership category later?

Yes. Members may request a category change if they become eligible for another membership classification, subject to Foundation policies.

Can I hold more than one membership category?

Generally, members are enrolled in one primary membership category. Additional roles or appointments may be available through Foundation programmes.

Do institutions appoint representatives?

Yes. Institutional members may designate representatives to participate in Foundation activities in accordance with applicable policies.

Is membership available internationally?

Yes. The Foundation welcomes qualified applicants from around the world who support its mission and objectives.
