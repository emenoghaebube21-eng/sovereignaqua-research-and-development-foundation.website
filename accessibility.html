Accessibility Statement

SovereignAqua Research & Development Foundation is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible and usable by everyone, regardless of ability or technology.

Our Commitment

We strive to provide a website that is inclusive, welcoming, and accessible to all visitors. Accessibility is an integral part of our mission to advance research, education, innovation, sustainability, and community engagement.

Accessibility Standards

Our goal is to conform as closely as practical to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA. These internationally recognized standards help make web content more accessible for people with disabilities.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML structure.
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation.
  • Visible keyboard focus indicators.
  • Alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Responsive layouts for mobile and desktop.
  • Support for screen readers.
  • High-contrast design elements.
  • ARIA attributes where appropriate.
  • Skip-to-content navigation.
  • Support for reduced-motion user preferences.

Known Limitations

While we continuously improve our website, some third-party content, embedded media, or legacy documents may not yet fully meet our accessibility standards. We are actively reviewing and improving these resources.

Feedback and Assistance

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of this website or need information in an alternative format, we encourage you to contact us.

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing process. We regularly review our website, test new content, and make improvements to provide an inclusive experience for all visitors.
