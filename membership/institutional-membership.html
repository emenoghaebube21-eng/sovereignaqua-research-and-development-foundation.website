ELIGIBILITY

Who May Apply?

Institutional Membership is open to legally established organizations whose mission, activities, or strategic objectives align with the Foundation's commitment to research, education, innovation, sustainability, and community development.

Legal Recognition

Applicants should be legally recognized organizations operating in accordance with the laws of their jurisdiction.

Mission Alignment

Organizations should demonstrate objectives compatible with the Foundation's mission and values.

Commitment to Collaboration

Applicants should be willing to engage constructively in collaborative programmes, projects, or knowledge-sharing activities.

RESPONSIBILITIES

Member Commitments

  • Support the Foundation's mission and strategic objectives.
  • Maintain professional and ethical standards during collaborative activities.
  • Respect applicable laws, policies, and intellectual property rights.
  • Promote transparency, accountability, and responsible governance.
  • Encourage respectful collaboration among participating organizations.
  • Share knowledge and expertise where appropriate to advance common goals.
APPLICATION

How to Apply

Step 1

Submit an Institutional Membership Application with organizational details and primary contact information.

Step 2

Provide supporting documentation relevant to the organization's activities and objectives, if requested.

Step 3

Applications are reviewed to assess alignment with the Foundation's mission, programmes, and collaboration goals.

Step 4

Approved organizations receive confirmation, onboarding information, and guidance on participation opportunities.

FAQ

Institutional Membership Questions

Can organizations from any country apply?

Yes. Applications from organizations worldwide are welcome, subject to the Foundation's membership policies and applicable legal requirements.

Is there an annual membership fee?

Any applicable membership fees, if adopted, will be communicated through the Foundation's official membership policies.

Can multiple representatives participate?

An Institutional Member may designate representatives in accordance with the Foundation's membership guidelines.

Can membership be reviewed or discontinued?

Institutional Membership remains subject to compliance with Foundation policies and may be reviewed periodically.
