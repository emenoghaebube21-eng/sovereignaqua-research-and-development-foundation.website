FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can become a member?

Membership is open to students, researchers, educators, professionals, institutions, nonprofit organizations, and individuals who support the Foundation's mission.

Are membership benefits available internationally?

Yes. The Foundation welcomes members from around the world, subject to its membership policies.

Can members participate in research projects?

Eligible members may participate in Foundation research initiatives according to project requirements and available opportunities.

How do I apply?

Complete the online membership application and follow the instructions provided during the review process.
