MEMBERSHIP POLICIES

Membership Standards & Governance

Our membership policies establish a fair, transparent, and professional framework that supports collaboration, ethical conduct, accountability, and the advancement of the Foundation's mission.

Purpose of These Policies

These Membership Policies define the eligibility requirements, responsibilities, privileges, and administrative procedures applicable to all members of the SovereignAqua Research & Development Foundation. They are intended to promote fairness, transparency, professionalism, and active participation throughout the Foundation.

Eligibility

Individuals

Students, researchers, educators, professionals, and individuals supporting the Foundation's objectives.

Organizations

Universities, research institutes, government agencies, nonprofit organizations, and private-sector partners.

Acceptance

Membership approval is subject to the Foundation's review procedures and applicable eligibility requirements.

Member Rights

  • Participate in approved Foundation programmes and activities.
  • Receive Foundation communications and updates.
  • Access member resources made available for their membership category.
  • Be treated fairly and respectfully.
  • Provide constructive feedback regarding Foundation initiatives.
  • Apply for eligible volunteer, committee, or leadership opportunities where applicable.

Member Responsibilities

  • Support the Foundation's mission and objectives.
  • Maintain respectful and professional conduct.
  • Comply with Foundation policies and applicable laws.
  • Provide accurate membership information.
  • Protect confidential information where required.
  • Promote ethical research and responsible collaboration.
  • Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose them when appropriate.
